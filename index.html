FAQ

      
Common questions

      
Everything clients usually ask before we start. Something else on your mind? Just WhatsApp me.

    

    

      

        How long does it take to build a website?
        
A starter site or landing page takes about a week. A full business website takes 2–3 weeks. E-commerce stores and custom web apps typically take 4–6 weeks depending on features. You'll see progress previews along the way, not just a final reveal.

      

      

        What do you need from me to get started?
        
Just the basics: your logo (or I can help create one), photos of your business or products, the text you want (or notes — I'll polish the wording), and one or two websites you like the look of. A short WhatsApp chat is usually enough to kick things off.

      

      

        How much do a domain and hosting cost?
        
A .co.ke or .com domain costs roughly $8–15 per year. Hosting for most business sites runs from free to a few dollars a month depending on your needs. I handle the entire setup for you and put everything in your name — you own it all.

      

      

        Can you add M-Pesa payments to my site?
        
Yes. The Premium package includes M-Pesa and card payment integration, so your customers can pay the way they already know. I also set up order notifications so you never miss a sale.

      

      

        How do payments work?
        
50% deposit to start, 50% when the site is done and you're happy with it. For Premium projects we can split it into three milestones. No hidden costs — the package price is what you pay.

      

      

        Can you improve or maintain my existing website?
        
Absolutely. I do redesigns, speed-ups, mobile-friendly makeovers and ongoing maintenance. Send me your current site on WhatsApp and I'll tell you exactly what I'd improve — free.
